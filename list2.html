These are my favorite albums by Hip Hop Artists in the 2020’s.

1. Wunna by Gunna

It was released in 2020 and is an astrology themed album, with "Wunna" representing an alter-ego.

2. Certified Lover Boy By Drake

According to Drake, this album is "A combination of toxic masculinity and acceptance of truth which is inevitably heartbreaking."

3. Her Loss by Drake and 21 Savage

This album plays into a triology of music Drake has produced, starting with Certified Lover Boy, leading to Honestly, Nevermind and ending with Her Loss.

4. Still Over It By Summer Walker

According to Summer Walker, “Take this opportunity to learn from my mistakes. You don’t have to guess if something is love. Love is shown through actions. Stop making excuses for people who don’t show up for you. Don’t ignore the red flags. And don’t think you have to stay somewhere ’cause you can’t find better—you can and you will. Don’t settle for less—you don’t deserve it and neither does your family.”

5. F*ck the World By Brent Faiyaz

According to Genius, "F*ck the World" covers theme "ranging from systemical racism to the overindulgence of sex and money in the world"

DS4EVER

6. DS4EVER is Gunna's third studio album. DS4EVER is the deluxe album of Gunna's Album, "DS4"
