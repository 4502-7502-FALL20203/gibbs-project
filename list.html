Best Black-owned food places in Chicago

This list includes the best and my favorite black-owned food places in Chicago, Illinois.


Best Black-owned food places in Chicago


Harold's Chicken

1208 E 53rd ST

(773)752-9270

yelp.com/biz/harolds-chicken-14-chicago.com

A Chicago staple. If you ask anyone from Chicago AND from the southside where to get the best mild sauce and chicken, they'll absolutely say Harold's Chicken!



Phlavz

717 W Maxwell St. Chicago, IL 60607

(773)717-7979

(773) 373–3530

https://aintshesweetcafe.com

A restaurant I hold close to my heart. They may only serve soups, salads, and sandwiches, but everything is made with love.



Marlon's Chicken

602 E 75th St Chicago, IL 60619 8

(773) 891-1619

yelp.com/biz/marlons-chicken-chicago-2

This is personally my absolute favorite place in the city. It's better than any other Mild Chicken places in the city.



Tropic Island Jerk Chicken

553 E 79th St Chicago, IL 60619

(773) 224-7766

Jamaican food is my favorite ethnic cuisine. This place will not fail you. The food takes 40 mins so you know it's good!



14 Parish

1644 E 53rd Street, Chicago, IL 60615

(312) 291-8379

14parish.com/

Slightly more upscale, but still delicious Jamaican food!



Cleo’s Southern Cuisine

4248 South Cottage Grove

(773) 633-2757

cleossoutherncuisine.com/

Soul Food. That's all the description needs to say.
